Technical troubleshooting

Try various browsers, attendees often find better success using Google Chrome. Clear your web browser cache, re-login to the event platform. Make sure you are not blocking cookies in your browser settings during this event – it can affect your ability to access certain features like changing your profile picture or joining a roundtable. Be sure you have closed out of other tabs in your browser for best performance.

What is UC Berkeley Global?

We are the continuing education school of UC Berkeley and bring Berkeley-quality education to our smaller class sizes. We offer everything you need to succeed: rigorous coursework, guidance from expert international student services staff, and events and activities designed to build strong academic and professional networks.

You'll learn from instructors who love teaching and are experienced professionals in their fields. Your classmates come from diverse backgrounds and help create engaging, ever-changing classroom discussions.

We are proud of the community we have created to welcome the bright and ambitious students arriving in Berkeley from all over the globe. Opportunities for personal and career development await you here at our world-renowned university in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Welcome to San Francisco

Immerse yourself in all the opportunities this vibrant, temperate, multicultural city has to offer, with beautiful views, access to nature and world-class events. The city and surrounding regions are home to beaches, hiking trails, museums, famed restaurants and so much more.

The city is also the northern neighbor of Silicon Valley, the tech and venture funding capital of the world. Companies from virtually every industry operate in the Bay Area, and many have offices in downtown San Francisco. You may walk by the site of your future internship on the way to class!
