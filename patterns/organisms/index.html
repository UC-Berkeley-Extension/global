What is UC Berkeley Global?

We are the continuing education school of UC Berkeley and bring Berkeley-quality education to our smaller class sizes. We offer everything you need to succeed: rigorous coursework, guidance from expert international student services staff, and events and activities designed to build strong academic and professional networks.

You'll learn from instructors who love teaching and are experienced professionals in their fields. Your classmates come from diverse backgrounds and help create engaging, ever-changing classroom discussions.

We are proud of the community we have created to welcome the bright and ambitious students arriving in Berkeley from all over the globe. Opportunities for personal and career development await you here at our world-renowned university in the San Francisco Bay Area.

Welcome to Berkeley

Life shaping. World changing. Future building. That's Berkeley. Imagine yourself here.

Data Analytics Full-Time Certificate

Learn to collect, process, analyze and interpret data in order to develop actionable business insights. Expand your skills by learning how to leverage statistics, data mining and big data to develop strategies in marketing, retail, health care, finance and other fields.

Knowledge of statistics (fulfill this prerequisite by taking Introduction to Statistics online).

  • Introduction to Data Analytics | COMPSCI X407.9
  • Introduction to Databases | COMPSCI X409.1
  • Data Analytics and Visualization | COMPSCI X465
  • Programming Python | COMPSCI X434
  • Career Development Fundamentals | BUS ADM 2054

  • Remote internship with a Bay Area company ($3,000 USD additional fee)
  • Certificate of completion

  • Understand statistics, coding, data mining, visualization and reporting.
  • Extract and analyze data using tools such as tables, charts, pivot tables, statistical analysis, R, Python, databases and SQL.

Data Science Full-Time Certificate

Learn to perform advanced data wrangling, data mining, statistical modeling and machine learning on data sets that may be very large and complex. Prepare for a data scientist role by analyzing large quantities of unstructured historical data to develop innovative solutions to complex business problems.

Completion of the

  • Data Analytics Full-Time Certificate

  • Introduction to Data Science | COMPSCI X415.2
  • Python for Data Analysis and Scientific Computing | COMPSCI X433.3
  • Introduction to Big Data | COMPSCI X409.2
  • Business Intelligence With SQL Server | EL ENG X404.8
  • Career Development Fundamentals | BUS ADM 2054

  • Remote internship with a Bay Area company ($3,000 USD additional fee)
  • Certificate of completion

  • Perform advanced data wrangling, data mining, statistical modeling and machine learning on large, complex data sets.
  • Run data collection and cleaning, modeling, evaluation, visualization, reporting and deployment.
  • Develop solutions using Spark, R, Python, TensorFlow, relational databases and SQL.

Data Science Full-Time Certificate

Build upon the Data Analytics Certification. Learn advanced data wrangling, statistical modeling and machine learning on complex data sets.

  • Duration: 1 semester, Fall or Spring
  • Cost: $8,000 USD
  • Prerequisite: Data Analytics Full-Time Certificate
LEARN MORE

Data Science Full-Time Professional Program

Complete the coursework in the Data Analytics and Data Science certificates, and take 4 additional semester units to complete this program.

  • Duration: 2 semesters
  • Cost: $22,000 (USD) + $1,000 (USD) per elective
LEARN MORE
  • Certificates without prerequisites can be taken individually.
  • The program structure is designed to meet the needs of visiting students on an F1 Visa.
  • Graduates of two semester long Full-Time Certificates meet the requirements to apply for OPT.
  • Students must have a minimum of three years of undergraduate coursework for admission.
  • International students must meet English Language Proficiency Requirements.
  • Fees include tuition, health insurance and student services.
